Depok inside

Date Created

25,01,2025

Purpose

Depok city information

Description

This website is designed as a center for factual information about the city of Depok, presenting a variety of content ranging from tourist destinations and culinary specialties to education and public services. With a modern design and user-friendly interface, this website aims to make it easier for the public and tourists to find accurate and reliable information. Through its comprehensive and structured presentation, this website is expected to become a primary reference source for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of Depok City—including its culture, potential, and available facilities. This design was created as part of a competition project, highlighting innovation in presenting city information through digital platforms.

Objective

-A primary source of information for residents and tourists who want to learn more about Depok.
-A promotional platform for local culinary SMEs and tourist destinations in Depok to gain wider recognition.
-An educational portal for students and newcomers seeking schools or healthcare services in Depok.
-Improving government transparency by providing news and public information in a clearer and more accessible way.

Key features

-Tourism Recommendations – Discover a well-curated selection of Depok’s tourist destinations, presented with detailed descriptions, exact locations, and engaging photos. This feature is designed to help both residents and visitors explore the city more easily and plan their trips more effectively.
-Culinary Highlights – Explore the rich flavors of Depok through a showcase of its most iconic local dishes. Each culinary recommendation highlights must-try foods that represent the city’s culture, providing both tourists and food enthusiasts with authentic dining experiences.
-Public School Directory – A complete and reliable directory of public schools in Depok, offering clear information to assist students, parents, and newcomers in finding suitable educational institutions. This feature ensures easier access to accurate educational resources within the city.
-Interactive Q&A Forum – A digital community space where users can interact, share experiences, and exchange questions and answers with others. To ensure safety and personalization, users are required to log in before accessing this feature, making discussions more secure and meaningful.

Tools

-Figma

Project outcome

The outcome of the Depok Inside project is a high-fidelity prototype website that provides factual information about Depok, covering tourism, culinary, education, and public services. With a modern and user-friendly design, this website is expected to become a main source of information for both residents and visitors, while showcasing how technology can enhance community connectivity, information transparency, and communication efficiency in Depok.
